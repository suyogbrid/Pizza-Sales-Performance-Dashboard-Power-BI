3. Short Description / Purpose

The Pizza Sales Insights Dashboard is a visually rich and analytical Power BI report built to explore sales performance across categories, sizes, and individual pizza types. It helps business owners, analysts, and restaurant managers understand customer demand patterns, identify best‑selling products, and make data‑driven decisions to optimize menu strategy and operations.


4. Tech Stack

• 📊 Power BI Desktop – Main platform for data visualization and report creation.

5. Data Source

- Order dates and times

This structure enables analysis of trends by day, month, product type, and customer ordering behavior.


5. Features / Highlights
Following the same structure as your example:

• Business Problem
Restaurants often struggle to understand which products drive revenue, which days generate the most orders, and how customer preferences shift across time. Without a clear analytical view, optimizing menus, staffing, and marketing becomes challenging.
Key questions such as:
- Which pizzas generate the highest revenue?
- What days and months are the busiest?
- Which categories and sizes perform best?
- What products should be promoted or discontinued?
… are difficult to answer using raw transactional data.


• Goal of the Dashboard
To deliver an interactive analytical tool that:
- Highlights sales performance across time, category, and product level.
- Helps managers identify top and bottom performers.
- Supports decisions related to menu optimization, pricing, and promotions.
- Reveals customer ordering patterns to improve operational planning.

• Business Impact & Insights
- Menu Optimization: Identify high‑performing pizzas and underperformers to adjust offerings.
- Marketing Strategy: Promote best sellers and create campaigns for slow‑moving items.
- Operational Efficiency: Understand peak days and months for better staffing and inventory planning.
- Customer Behavior Analysis: Insights into preferred categories, sizes, and ordering patterns.
